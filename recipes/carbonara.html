Carbonara Dish

Description

Spaghetti alla carbonara. Luscious and wonderfully indulgent, pasta carbonara takes as long to make as it does to cook the pasta. Carbonara is a delicious traditional Italian pasta dish.

Ingrediants

  • Pancetta
  • Pasta Water
  • Eggs
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Parmesean

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of Water
  2. Brown the Pancetta
  3. Mix the eggs and parmesean and black Pepper
  4. Cook the pasta al dente
  5. add the pasta dripping wet to the egg mixture and toss vigoursly
  6. Add back the pancetta and s&p to taste
  7. Enjoy!
