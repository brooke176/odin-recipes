Garlic Bread

Description

Garlic bread is a delectable carb-filled wonder.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Herbs (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200
  2. Lay out sliced bread on a baking sheet
  3. Unpeel 20 cloves of garlic
  4. Put garlic cloves in a bowl and cover completely with olive oil
  5. Cook garlic mixture in oven for 30 minutes
  6. Take mixture out of oven and raise temp to 375
  7. Scoop 3-4 cloves of the cooked garlic along with 1-2 tbsp of the oil onto each slice of bread and spread the garlic throughout each piece
  8. Put baking sheet of bread into the oven and cook for 10 minutes
  9. Take garlic bread out of oven and top with chopped herbs
  10. Enjoy!
